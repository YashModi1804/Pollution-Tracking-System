About

Air pollution is one of the greatest global challenges of our time, contributing to severe health, environmental, and economic impacts. Pollutants such as particulate matter (PM2.5, PM10), nitrogen oxides (NOx), sulfur dioxide (SO2), carbon monoxide (CO), and ozone (O3) significantly affect air quality, making real-time tracking and predictive analytics crucial.

This cloud-based platform leverages artificial intelligence and satellite data to monitor and predict air pollution levels using Google Earth Engine. The system helps researchers, policymakers, and environmentalists gain actionable insights to mitigate the adverse effects of air pollution.

How This Application Helps

By integrating satellite observations, machine learning models, and cloud-based computational power, this application:

  • Predicts real-time aerosol concentrations and air quality over specific regions.
  • Supports decision-making for urban planning, environmental policy, and public health initiatives.
  • Facilitates educational and research efforts by providing accessible, processed data through Visualization.
  • Enhances early-warning systems for pollution-related health risks. [In Development]
